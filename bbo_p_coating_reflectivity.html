Single-Layer Protective-Coating Reflectivity

Single-layer protective coating on optical substrates, modeled with thin-film Fresnel interference. Center wavelength sets the quarter-wave coating thickness.

Inputs

For anisotropic crystals, the axis selector chooses one principal Sellmeier index. Surface Fresnel polarization is separate: choose p-polarized light to see the Brewster-angle minimum. The coated curve does not generally reach zero at the uncoated substrate Brewster angle because the film adds another interface.
